Our Story

Founded in 2004, LuxeHome Decor & Styling has been at the forefront of residential interior design. What started as a passion project by our founder, acclaimed designer Elena Martinez, has grown into a full-service design studio serving clients across the nation.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way you live, work, and relax at home.

Our Values

  • Passion: We love what we do and it shows in every project we undertake.
  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in every detail, from concept to completion.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with clients to bring their unique vision to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer?
We offer complete home styling, modern & luxury decor, space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, wall art curation, bedroom styling, kitchen & dining decor, and custom furniture design.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope. A single room can take 2-4 weeks, while complete home styling typically takes 2-3 months from consultation to final installation.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer flexible pricing based on project scope. Initial consultations are free, and we provide detailed quotes after understanding your needs and budget.
Do you work with existing furniture?
Absolutely! We can incorporate your existing pieces into the new design or help you select new furniture that complements what you already have.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve the New York metropolitan area, but we also take on select projects nationwide. Contact us to discuss your location.

Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

1. General Policy

At LuxeHome Decor & Styling, we strive for complete client satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines our commitment to fair and transparent refund practices.

2. Design Services Refunds

Consultation Fees

  • Free initial consultations are non-refundable (as they are complimentary)
  • Paid consultation fees are refundable within 24 hours of booking
  • No refunds after the consultation has been conducted

Design Development

  • 50% refund available if canceled before initial concepts are presented
  • 25% refund if canceled after first presentation but before revisions
  • No refund once revisions have begun or final designs are delivered

Project Management Fees

  • Refundable on a prorated basis for services not yet rendered
  • No refund for completed phases of work

3. Product Purchase Refunds

Furniture and Decor Items

  • Custom-ordered items are non-refundable once manufacturing begins
  • Stock items may be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging
  • Client responsible for return shipping costs
  • Restocking fee of 15% may apply

Non-Refundable Products

  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Items damaged due to client misuse
  • Fabric, wallpaper, or other materials cut to specification

4. Installation Services

  • Full refund if canceled at least 48 hours before scheduled installation
  • 50% refund if canceled within 24-48 hours
  • No refund for same-day cancellations
  • Rescheduling allowed once at no charge

5. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us via email at refunds@luxehome.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567
  2. Provide your order/project number and reason for refund request
  3. Include photos if claiming product defect or damage
  4. Wait for our team to review your request (typically within 2-3 business days)

Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Bank processing may take an additional 3-10 business days

6. Product Defects and Damages

Manufacturer Defects

  • Full refund or replacement for items with manufacturer defects
  • Must be reported within 30 days of delivery
  • Manufacturer warranty terms apply

Shipping Damage

  • Report damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of damage and packaging
  • Full refund or replacement provided
  • We handle all shipping claims

7. Deposits and Down Payments

  • Deposits secure your place in our project schedule
  • Deposits are non-refundable once work commences
  • Deposits may be transferred to future projects within 6 months
  • Full project cancellation before work starts: 50% deposit refund

8. Exceptions and Special Cases

We understand that circumstances change. We may make exceptions for:

  • Medical emergencies (documentation required)
  • Relocation due to job transfer (proof of employment change)
  • Natural disasters affecting your property
  • Other extraordinary circumstances (reviewed case-by-case)

9. Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to your satisfaction:

  • We'll work with you to address any concerns
  • Minor adjustments included in our service
  • We stand behind the quality of our work
  • Your happiness is our priority
