About LuxeHome

Our Story

Founded in 2004, LuxeHome Decor & Styling has been at the forefront of residential interior design. What started as a passion project by our founder, acclaimed designer Elena Martinez, has grown into a full-service design studio serving clients across the nation.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way you live, work, and relax at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer?
We offer complete home styling, modern & luxury decor, space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, wall art curation, bedroom styling, kitchen & dining decor, and custom furniture design.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope. A single room can take 2-4 weeks, while complete home styling typically takes 2-3 months from consultation to final installation.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer flexible pricing based on project scope. Initial consultations are free, and we provide detailed quotes after understanding your needs and budget.
Do you work with existing furniture?
Absolutely! We can incorporate your existing pieces into the new design or help you select new furniture that complements what you already have.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve the New York metropolitan area, but we also take on select projects nationwide. Contact us to discuss your location.
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Yes! We offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote services or are located outside our primary service area.
