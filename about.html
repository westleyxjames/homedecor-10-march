About LuxeHome

Our Story

Founded in 2004, LuxeHome Decor & Styling has been at the forefront of residential interior design. What started as a passion project by acclaimed designer Elena Martinez has grown into a full-service design studio serving clients nationwide.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way you live, work, and relax at home.
