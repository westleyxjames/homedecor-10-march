Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. General Policy

At LuxeHome Decor & Styling, we strive for complete client satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines our commitment to fair and transparent refund practices for all our services and products.

2. Design Services Refunds

Consultation Fees

  • Free initial consultations are non-refundable (as they are complimentary)
  • Paid consultation fees are refundable within 24 hours of booking
  • No refunds after the consultation has been conducted
  • Rescheduling is allowed once at no additional charge

Design Development

  • 50% refund available if canceled before initial concepts are presented
  • 25% refund if canceled after first presentation but before revisions begin
  • No refund once revisions have begun or final designs are delivered
  • All completed work remains property of LuxeHome

Project Management Fees

  • Refundable on a prorated basis for services not yet rendered
  • No refund for completed phases of work
  • Administrative fees of $250 apply to all cancellations

3. Product Purchase Refunds

Furniture and Decor Items

  • Custom-ordered items are non-refundable once manufacturing begins
  • Stock items may be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact
  • Client responsible for return shipping costs
  • Restocking fee of 15% may apply
  • Final sale items are non-refundable

Non-Refundable Products

  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Items damaged due to client misuse or negligence
  • Fabric, wallpaper, or other materials cut to specification
  • Special order items from manufacturers

4. Installation Services

  • Full refund if canceled at least 48 hours before scheduled installation
  • 50% refund if canceled within 24-48 hours of installation
  • No refund for same-day cancellations
  • Rescheduling allowed once at no charge with 24 hours notice
  • Emergency cancellations considered on a case-by-case basis

5. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us via email at refunds@luxehome.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567
  2. Provide your order/project number and reason for refund request
  3. Include photos if claiming product defect or damage
  4. Wait for our team to review your request (typically within 2-3 business days)
  5. Receive approval and refund instructions

Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Bank processing may take an additional 3-10 business days
  • You will receive email confirmation once refund is processed

6. Product Defects and Damages

Manufacturer Defects

  • Full refund or replacement for items with manufacturer defects
  • Must be reported within 30 days of delivery
  • Manufacturer warranty terms apply
  • We handle all warranty claims on your behalf

Shipping Damage

  • Report damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of damage and original packaging
  • Full refund or replacement provided at no cost
  • We handle all shipping claims with carriers

7. Deposits and Down Payments

  • Deposits secure your place in our project schedule
  • Deposits are non-refundable once work commences
  • Deposits may be transferred to future projects within 6 months
  • Full project cancellation before work starts: 50% deposit refund
  • Deposits forfeited after 6 months of inactivity

8. Exceptions and Special Cases

We understand that circumstances change. We may make exceptions for:

  • Medical emergencies (documentation required)
  • Relocation due to job transfer (proof of employment change)
  • Natural disasters affecting your property
  • Death in the immediate family
  • Other extraordinary circumstances (reviewed case-by-case)

9. Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to your satisfaction:

  • We'll work with you to address any concerns before considering refunds
  • Minor adjustments included in our service at no additional cost
  • We stand behind the quality of our work
  • Your happiness is our priority
  • 100% satisfaction guaranteed on workmanship

10. No Refund Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after project completion
  • Failure to provide access for installation
  • Client-caused delays exceeding 90 days
  • Violations of our Terms and Conditions
  • Unauthorized modifications to our designs or installations

11. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Email: refunds@luxehome.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Address: 123 Design Avenue, New York, NY 10001
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
