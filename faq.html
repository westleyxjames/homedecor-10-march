Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer? ▼
We offer complete home styling, modern & luxury decor, space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, wall art curation, bedroom styling, kitchen & dining decor, and custom furniture design. Each service is tailored to your specific needs and style preferences.
How long does a typical project take? ▼
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room redesign typically takes 2-4 weeks from consultation to completion, while complete home styling projects usually take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.
What is your pricing structure? ▼
Our pricing is project-based and depends on the scope of work, square footage, and your specific requirements. Initial consultations are free, and we provide detailed quotes after understanding your vision and budget. Design fees typically range from $2,000 to $50,000 depending on project size and complexity.
Do you work with existing furniture? ▼
Absolutely! We love incorporating pieces that hold special meaning to you. Our designers are skilled at blending existing furniture with new elements to create a cohesive, fresh look. We'll assess what to keep, what to repurpose, and what new pieces to add.
What areas do you serve? ▼
We primarily serve the New York metropolitan area and surrounding regions. However, we also take on select projects nationwide and offer virtual consultations for clients outside our primary service area. Contact us to discuss your location.
Do you offer virtual consultations? ▼
Yes! We offer comprehensive virtual consultations that include video calls, digital mood boards, 3D renderings, and detailed shopping lists. Virtual design services are perfect for clients who prefer remote collaboration or are located outside our service area.
What is included in a free consultation? ▼
Our complimentary consultation includes a discussion of your vision, style preferences, functional needs, budget, and timeline. We'll assess your space, provide initial recommendations, answer all your questions, and outline the next steps to bring your design to life.
Can I see examples of your previous work? ▼
Yes! We have an extensive portfolio showcasing our completed projects across various styles and budgets. During your consultation, we'll share relevant examples that align with your aesthetic preferences and project type. We're proud of our work and love showing it off!
Do you handle project management and installation? ▼
Yes! We provide full-service project management from start to finish. This includes coordination with contractors, procurement of all materials and furnishings, delivery scheduling, professional installation, and final styling. You can relax while we handle every detail.
What if I'm not happy with the design? ▼
Your satisfaction is our top priority. Our design process includes multiple revision rounds, and we won't proceed to the next phase until you're completely happy with the direction. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our work and will make it right if something doesn't meet your expectations.
